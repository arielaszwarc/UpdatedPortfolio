About Me

Artist/Writer

Ariela Szwarc is a writer and abstract painter based in Northern New Jersey. She has been drawn to the arts ever since she can remember. Ariela is a lover of storytelling and all things whimsicial and ecentric. From a young age, Ariela has been passionate about writing. She has been honing her skills and exploring the craft since. Ariela has written an array of copy, essays, and journalistic material. Through her paintings, Ariela is focused on creating bold colorful work expoloring psychology, sexuality, childhood, and the feminine form. The limitless nature of written and visual communication has allowed Ariela to have an intimate journey with her writing and art.
